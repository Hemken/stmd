Theme Breaks (horizontal rules)

Three or more dashes, underscores, or asterisks. May be preceded by up to three spaces.

Written as:

---
___
***

Rendered as:

Too few:

–

__

**

Interspersed with spaces:

- - -
_ _ _
* * *

Theme break in a paragraph? No. (Underscores and dashes become headers.)

standard
***
deviation

standard *** deviation

Break in a blockquote? No. (Underscores and dashes become headers.)

> quoted
> ***
> text

quoted *** text

Break in a list? Yes. Notice using the same character as a list and as a theme break yields a pure theme break.

- item 1
- ***
- ---
- ___
- item 2
* ***
  • item 1
  • item 2
