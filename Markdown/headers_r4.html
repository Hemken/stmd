Some Examples of Headers

  1. Denoted with "underscores" (actually, at least 3 equal signs or dashes on the next line of text). Must be preceded by a blank line. Written:
Header 1
========
Header 2
---

Rendered:

Header 1

Header 2

  1. Denoted with "hash" marks ("#") and a space, before the header text. Up to six levels of headers are supported in Stata. No preceding blank line required in this style. Written:
# Header 1
## Header 2
### Header 3
#### Header 4
##### Header 5
###### Header 6
####### Header 7

Rendered:

Header 1

Header 2

Header 3

Header 4

Header 5
Header 6

####### Header 7

  1. Headers may appear within

    • Blockquotes. Written:
    > ### Quoted header

     Rendered:

    Quoted header

  • Lists (but not in R's sundown), written:

    * ### Item 1
* ### Item 2

    Rendered:

    • Item 1

    • Item 2
